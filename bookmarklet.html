OG Entity Encode

What is this?

This is a bookmarklet that makes it easier to post content on the OG in non-European languages.

Instructions

  1. Move your mouse over "OG Entity Encode" and click and drag it to your bookmarks bar on your browser.
  2. Go to a thread and write some content in a non-European language.
  3. Highlight the non-European text in the textarea.
  4. Click on the bookmarklet.

You should see that all the highlighted text has been turned into HTML entities.

Note that this only works in the framed mode, and it has only been tested in Chrome and Firefox.
